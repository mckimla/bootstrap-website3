Who Are We?

The Virginia Repertory Dance Ensemble is James Madison Universityâ€™s pre-professional performing company. The audition based company performs the work of nationally known guest artists and faculty members in a mainstage concert each year. The company also tours across Virginia in the spring season to perform work and teach master classes. The current company is composed of sixteen junior and senior dance majors. The directors of the company are Ryan Corriston and Matt Pardo, with dramaturg Zachary Dorsey.

VRDC'S Mission Statement:

We are explorers. We are builders. We are a collective of young artists in motion, diverse in experience and background, united in purpose. We are VRDC '20-'21.
