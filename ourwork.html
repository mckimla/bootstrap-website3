Our Work

vessel by Emily Cargill

This piece was set on VRDC in October, 2019. The performers are Hailey Clevenger, Haley Labby, Chloe Conway, Raeanna Grey, and Julianna Raimondo. The dancers explore the surroundings with a delicate precision in moments of movement together and alone.
The music is With the Gift of Your Small Breath, Akira Rabelaisand mad by CoH & Cosey Fanni Tutti and photos are taken by Richard Finkelstein.

Dissolution by Jess Burgess

Jess Burgess set this piece on the entire company in February, 2019. The performers are Ashton Clevenger, Hailey Clevenger, Chloe Conway, Lindsey McKim, Ammara Shafqat, Molly Philpott, Haley Labby, Raeanna Grey, Gregory Pergerson, Maddie Collins, Maddie Counsell, Julianna Raimondo, Sophie Witmer, Jessica Eurom, Grace Mayer, and Ana Hart. The work investigates the hidden energy that keeps a group together, and the battle they must wage to maintain their connectivity.
The music is by Khandig Ray and photos are by Richard Finkelstein.

Much Further Out by Stephanie Liapis

This work was set on VRDC dancers in October, 2019. The performers are Lindsey McKim, Ashton Clevenger, Jessica Eurom, Ana Hart, Molly Philpott, Sophie Witmer, and Maddie Collins. Much Further Out signifies a world which attempted to explain how the nation’s wealth of gun violence and adoration for Stephanie's newborn baby could equally evoke an overwhelming feeling of helplessness in sadness and in love.
The music is composed by Martin Jarmick and photos are by Richard Finkelstein.

Dust on a Rose by Ryan Corriston

Ryan Corriston created this work in collaboration with the dancers throughout the fall semester in 2019. The dancers are Ammara Shafqat, Grace Mayer, and Maddie Counsell. This is a piece that explores our inner journey. It reflects on ideas of nostalgia, disappointment, anger, but most of all hope.
The music is Sunny Field With Birds by Organic Nature Sounds and This Bitter Earth by Max Richter and Dinah Washington and photos are by Richard Finkelstein.
