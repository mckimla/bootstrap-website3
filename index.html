Artistic Statement

As a dancer, I strive to create art that is meaningful, compelling, inventive, and unique. Creating a piece allows me to tell a story through movement and allows audiences to relate, react, and respond to that story. Dance serves as a means of communication and can resonate with people from various communities and backgrounds. Dance is universal and can breakthrough all language, ability, and mental barriers. Dance is a means of expression and communication. A successful piece for me is one that leaves an impact, whether that be positive or negative. A piece that leaves audience members wondering, analyzing, and responding is a success. For the future, I want to create art that leaves an impact. Art that allows audiences to think. Art that is accessible to people with different backgrounds and abilities.
